Frequently Asked Questions

What is an analysis token?

Each token allows you to perform one complete SaaS analysis, including market research, pain point identification, and opportunity recommendations.

How long are tokens valid?

Tokens validity varies by package - from 6 months for Starter Pack to 18+ months for larger packs. Unused tokens expire after their validity period.

Can I buy more tokens later?

Yes! You can purchase additional token packs at any time. Tokens from different packs maintain their respective features and validity periods.

What happens if I need more features?

You can upgrade to a higher tier token pack at any time. The new tokens will include all features of the higher tier package.
